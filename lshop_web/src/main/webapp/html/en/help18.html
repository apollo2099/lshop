Track：

How to check my orderstatus?

1.If you are registered users, you could check your order status in “CustomerCenter” after you login.  

2.It doesn't matter if you have not registered yet for the system willautomatically assign you a login ID (the E-mail address you input when placingyour order) and password (the initial password will be sent to your reserved e-mail)when you place your order. You may use the login ID to check your order statusin “Customer Center”.

3.You can also ask our customer service to check the order status for youby providing your order No. and delivery details.

Note: Unpaid orders are valid for only a month. We will remove all unpaidorders of last month on the last day of every month (for example, on February29th, we will clear all unpaid orders from Jan1th-31th.

Modify：

How to modify order details?

The order details (order quantity, consignee, billing address,etc.) can be modified in “Customer Center” or via customer service before youpay. However, once you have made payment for your order, you are not allowed tomodify your order details.

Comment：

How to comment on products?

After receipt of your package, you can make comments on our products,service, etc. after order confirmation. Also, you can visit our official forumTVpad Fans (http://www.tvpadfans.com)to share your shopping experience, and there will be more pleasant surprisewaiting for you!
